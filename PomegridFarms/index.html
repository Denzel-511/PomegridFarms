Who we are

At Pomegrid Aqua, we are committed to providing our customers with the freshest and most delicious tilapia and catfish on the market. FAQ

What types of fish do you offer?

We offer fresh tilapia and catfish, sustainably raised in our freshwater ponds. Both are free from hormones and antibiotics.

What makes Pomegrid Aqua different from other fish farms?

At Pomegrid Aqua, we pride ourselves on our commitment to sustainability and ethical farming practices. We believe in raising our fish in clean, freshwater environments, without the use of hormones or antibiotics. Additionally, we offer fast, reliable shipping and exceptional customer service.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes, we offer free delivery for orders over 100cedis. For orders under 100cedis, a fee will be added to your order total.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply visit our Products page, select your desired fish and quantity, and proceed to checkout. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.

What is your quality guarantee?

At Pomegrid Aqua, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you are not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery and we will do our best to make it right.
